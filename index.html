The factorial of a number is denoted as n!

It is calculated by n! = n * (n-1) * (n-2) * ... * 1

A common use case for factorials is to calculate arrangements and possibilities. Say, for example, you have a standard deck of 52 cards and you wanted to know the amount of unique ways you can arrange them. The answer would be the factorial of 52, or 52! (which is an indredibly large number ðŸ˜µ).
